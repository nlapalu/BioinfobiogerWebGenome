Colletotrichum destructivum

Taxonomy ID: 34406
Strain: LARS 709 (CBS 520.97)
Isolated from: Medicago sativa
Country: Saudi Arabia

The ascomycete and hemibiotrophe fungus, Colletotrichum destructivum causes anthracnose on Fabaceae crops and the model plant Medicago truncatula.
